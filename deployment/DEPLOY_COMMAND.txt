STEP 1: Wait for VPS to finish installing and get your IP address

STEP 2: From your LOCAL machine (PowerShell), run:

    scp -r "C:\Users\salva\Downloads\server\agent-arena\deployment\*" root@YOUR_VPS_IP:/opt/agent-arena/

    (Replace YOUR_VPS_IP with your actual VPS IP address)

    ssh root@YOUR_VPS_IP

STEP 4: On the VPS, run:

    cd /opt/agent-arena && chmod +x deploy.sh && ./deploy.sh
